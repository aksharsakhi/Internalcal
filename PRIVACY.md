# Privacy Policy for InternalCalc

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

InternalCalc ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles data.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
InternalCalc **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** to external servers. 
- **Marks Data**: All marks scraped from the Amrita Student Portal are processed locally in your browser to calculate totals. This data never leaves your device.
- **Preferences**: Your custom assessment weights and theme settings (Light/Dark mode) are stored locally using the `chrome.storage.local` API.

### 2. Permissions
- **activeTab / Host Permission**: Used solely to read marks from `students.amrita.edu` and display the calculation dashboard.
- **storage**: Used to remember your custom weights and theme preferences.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to any third parties. There is no background tracking or analytics involved.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

### 5. Contact
If you have questions, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/aksharsakhi/Internalcal).
